# In-World Crafting

GroovyScript adds the ability to craft things in-world via specific processes. By default, no recipes are created and everything must be added by you through GroovyScript.

## Categories

Has 6 subcategories.

* [Burning Conversion](./burning.md)

* [Explosion Conversion](./explosion.md)

* [Fluid and ItemStack To Block Conversion](./fluid_to_block.md)

* [Fluid and ItemStack To Fluid Conversion](./fluid_to_fluid.md)

* [Fluid and ItemStack To ItemStack Conversion](./fluid_to_item.md)

* [Piston Pushing Conversion](./piston_push.md)
